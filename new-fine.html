Who We Are

Digital & Virtual Incorporated T/A as (D&V Chartered Accountants) is a professional consulting, audit, advisory and accounting services company that provides hands-on support to public, private and non-profit organizations.

Our services have been designed to address the special needs of a wide range of government and non-government organization. Xabacha is striving for excellence and quality in every service and product we offer, our various service lines are led by qualified and experienced various professional and chartered accountants who are experts in their fields of service.

What We Do

Our Vision

To grow our company to offer and accommodate a broader spectrum of specialist services, providing efficient and excellent services, and becoming our clients' preferred service provider of choice.

Our Mission

To develop our team and provide a leading platform in accordance with statutory practices, codes of conduct, and integrity, to deliver overall outstanding service to our valued clients.

Services

ACCOUNTING SERVICES

Our accounting services team provides expert guidance on financial statement preparation, budgeting, forecasting, and financial analysis. We help our clients to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements, improve financial reporting, and make informed business decisions.

TAX SERVICES

Our tax services team provides expert advice and guidance on Income tax, VAT, and other indirect taxes. We help our clients to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements, optimize tax planning, and minimize tax liabilities.

AUDIT & ASSURANCE

Our audit and assurance services are designed to provide independent and objective assurance on the accuracy and reliability of financial statements. We help our clients to identify and mitigate risks, improve internal controls, and optimize business processes.

ADVISORY SERVICES

We provide expert guidance on risk assessment, risk mitigation, and risk monitoring. Helping businesses with objective assurance and consulting services to help clients achieve their strategic objectives.
